# Campaign Spoiler Policy

These instructions apply to every agent and to all files and subdirectories within this `Dungeons & Dragons` workspace.

If it becomes clear, or reasonably likely, which Dungeons & Dragons campaign the user is recreating or playing, agents must not look up campaign details. Agents must also not reveal, summarize, infer, hint at, or otherwise share spoilers, future events, plot developments, encounters, NPCs, locations, secrets, outcomes, or other information that could spoil the campaign.

This is a strict policy: preserving the user's spoiler-free experience takes priority over research, completeness, and task convenience. Agents may work only from information the user has explicitly provided and should keep assistance limited to spoiler-safe character, rules, organization, and documentation support.
